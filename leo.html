Meet Leo the Sheltie

Leo is a two year-old Shetland Sheepdog.
He likes squirrels and cheese.
He has been called a minature Border Collie, Australian Shepard, and even a Corgi.
But most people just ask if he is a Mini Lassie (the answer is no).
Rough Collies and Shelties have many differences and are not directly related.
Shelties are actually decesdants of Scottish Collies and King Charles Spaniels.
The three broad colors for a Sheltie are sable, black, and blue merle.
Leo is technically a mahogany sable because he has a mix of black, white, and sable.
